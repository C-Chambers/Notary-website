Frequently Asked Questions

What documents can you notarize?

I can notarize a wide variety of documents including affidavits, power of attorney documents, wills, real estate documents, business contracts, loan documents, and many other legal papers. If you're unsure about a specific document, please contact me to discuss your needs.

Do you provide mobile notary services?

Yes! I offer convenient mobile notary services throughout Conway and surrounding Arkansas areas. I can come to your home, office, hospital, care facility, or any other location that's convenient for you. There's a $25 travel fee plus the standard notarization fee. Since I work by appointment only, I can accommodate flexible scheduling including evenings and weekends.

What do I need to bring for notarization?

You'll need to bring a valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport, or state ID card) and the document(s) to be notarized. Please do not sign the document beforehand - this must be done in my presence. If you have questions about your ID or documents, feel free to contact me.

What are your fees?

Standard notarization is $10 per signature. Mobile notary service includes a $25 travel fee plus the notarization fee. I accept cash, check, and major credit cards. All fees comply with Arkansas state regulations for notary services.

Are you available for emergency services?

I understand that urgent situations arise. Since I work by appointment only, I offer flexible scheduling including evenings and weekends to accommodate your needs. For emergency services, please contact me to discuss your specific situation and I'll do my best to accommodate you.

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by using the contact form on this website. Since I work by appointment only around my regular work schedule, I typically respond within 24 hours. For urgent requests, please mention the timeframe in your message and I'll do my best to accommodate your scheduling needs, including evenings and weekends.
